
The cube is breathing.
As anxious thoughts fill its mind, the world around it seems to shrink.
The breathing continues, becoming more rapid and sporadic.
Then, a sphere rushes in next to the cube to comfort it.
The cube is brought back to reality and breathes one final sigh of relief.

Principles used:
- squash and stretch
- staging
- timing

Emotions meant to be conveyed:
- anxiety
- depression
- claustrophobia
- isolation
- compassion
- relief
